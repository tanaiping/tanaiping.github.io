SUORIN

Brand origin

As the core business and brand of Youme, showed a distinctive brand positioning when the first product was launched. While pen-style e-cigarettes and big-clouds e-cigarette devices were popular in the market, Suorin released a card-style e-cigarette device that fit better in your pocket and became popular worldwide.

The Story of Suorin

Founded on the belief that top quality vape product will help people quit smoking and experience heath benefits, Suorin was established in 2016. Equipped with a strong technology research & development center and with highly skilled and professional teams, until now, Suorin has made its mark as a leading brand of E-Cigarette around the world. Suorin products are exported to more than 90 countries.

First card-style design

Focus on the pod system device R&D

Suorin introduced the Air Kit in 2016. The first card-style vape device showcasing a much thinner than any other product of the time.

Since its establishment, Suorin has focused on the development and manufacture of pod system vape device, hoping that Suorin fans will stand out from the crowd of e-cigarette enthusiasts through the unique appearance of the product design.
