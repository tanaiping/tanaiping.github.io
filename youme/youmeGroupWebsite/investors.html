Why Youme Global

01 01

Large and Rapidly Growing Markets

E-vapor and cannabis vaping markets are expected to reach $29.9 billion and $11.6 billion in 2024. E-vapor – Continue to be driven by the secular trend of healthier tobacco alternatives. Cannabis vaping – Favorable political environment coupled with the rise of popularity of cannabis derivatives.

02 02

Industry Leading Innovations

As the pioneer of the “cloud chasing” movement, Aspire has had a history of disruptive vaping technologies. Over 200 patents issued and over 800 patents pending across China, the U.S., E.U. and other countries.

03 03

Integrated Design/Production Capabilities

Implementation of disruptive product designs enabled by highly capable manufacturing expertise. Capitalizing on the proven success in self-branded products to expand into ODM solutions.

04 04

Globally Recognized Consumer Brand

Serial winner of various renowned vaping rewards voted by users, including ecc, VTC, NVE, among others. Full-stack marketing initiatives have generated traction across platforms such as Instagram, Facebook and YouTube.

05 05

Deep Bench of Industry Veterans

Senior management team has a combined experience of over 50 years in consumer electronics with track record of innovation and execution in the e-vapor products industry.
